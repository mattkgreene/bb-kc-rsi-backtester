- Capital/Risk: w_cash, w_commission, w_trade_mode, w_use_stop, w_stop_mode, w_stop_pct, w_stop_atr_mult, w_use_trailing, w_trail_pct, w_max_bars_in_trade, w_daily_loss_limit, w_risk_per_trade_pct
